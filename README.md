### 1. The Concept
**"The 90% Benchmark: From Scanning to Career Strategy"**

**CareerBoost-AI** is a career development platform that challenges the traditional resume-building process. It begins with a strict **ATS Diagnostic Scan**; if your resume scores below **90%**, the system provides a precise "Adjustment Roadmap" to refine your profile until the benchmark is hit. 

Once the **90% threshold** is achieved, the platform unlocks its strategic engine to align your profile with the **best-fit roles in the Saudi Labor Market**. It doesn't just stop at scoring; it identifies your **Critical Gaps** in skills or certifications. If a certification is missing, it provides the **official link** to obtain it. To solidify your expertise, it generates a **Project-Based Plan**, suggesting specific real-world projects designed to build and prove the exact skills you need to dominate the local market.

---

### 2. User Journey

#### **Phase A: The 90% Challenge (Optimization)**
1.  **Upload:** The user uploads their resume in **PDF** format.
2.  **ATS Diagnostic:** The system calculates the **ATS Score**.
3.  **Refinement Loop:** * If the score is **below 90%**, the AI generates a "Fix-List" of necessary edits.
    * The user updates their resume and re-uploads until the **90% benchmark** is met.

#### **Phase B: Market Alignment (Strategy)**
4.  **Role Identification:** Once the 90% threshold is cleared, the system identifies the most relevant **Job Titles** based on the user's profile and current **Saudi Market trends**.
5.  **Deep Market Scan:** The AI analyzes the specific responsibilities and requirements for these roles within the **Saudi labor market**.
6.  **The Action Plan:** The system provides a comprehensive report including:
    * **Full Job Description:** Detailed tasks expected in the local market.
    * **Project Roadmap:** Targeted project suggestions to build and demonstrate required skills.
    * **Professional Certifications:** A list of essential certificates with direct links to their **Official Documentation**.
